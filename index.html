About HighperDao

HighperDAO is a decentralised incubator & launchpad that helps promising projects achieve their full potential by leveraging the investment power, launchpad and buzz making potential of our community.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to decentralised access to tailored investment opportunities & incubation program that were previously available only to well-heeled investors and venture capital, opening up the closed ecosystem of early-stage crypto project investments to everybody.

HighperDao is designed to help fuel the future of mass-market blockchain applications. We aim to be the best decentralized launchpad & incubator attracting & helping projects success.

hopes to break the pattern of early-phase interests in high-potential crypto projects, which commonly see huge conventional VCs purchase out piles of tokens in strategic, seed, and private sale investment rounds at far lower costs than retail investor during the public sale. These VCs would then dump their big bags on retail investor to get easy profits, while at the same time damaging the project
